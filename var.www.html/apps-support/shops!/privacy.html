Data Privacy

This app stores all its information in a local database. None of this data is sent over the Internet unless the user decides to share it with the appropriate app functionalites.

Usage Tracking

This app uses Localytics and Google Analytics, an analytics service provided by third parties. The usage data is anonymous and will be transmitted to and stored by Localytics and Google on servers in the United States.

Localytics and Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating the use of the app, compiling reports on app activity and providing other services relating to app activity.

Localytics and Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Localytics and Google's behalf.

By using this app, you consent to the processing of data about you by Localytics and Google in the manner and for the purposes set out above.
